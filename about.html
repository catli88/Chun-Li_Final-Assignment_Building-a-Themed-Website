Caring for the Environment

We believe in taking good care of the environment. As such, we have adopted ‘green initiatives’ including the utilization of environmentally friendly electric cooking systems in our kitchen. In doing our part to create a greener environment, we have planted lots of trees and flowers around the Resort.
